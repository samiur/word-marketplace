About the team

Tessa (Tess) Srebro

While working on her Master of Nonprofit Leadership degree at Seattle University, Tess recognized that she could use her love of writing and words to serve nonprofits. And she got really excited. So, after hearing about the vision of The Marketplace of Words, Tess jumped at the opportunity to create what you see here today. Find her on Twitter, LinkedIn or send an e-mail.

Erica Mills

Erica Mills envisioned a tool where nonprofits could access data on word usage to make informed decisions about their word choice.

Samiur Rahman

When brainstorming how to collect the data for The Marketplace of Words, Tess realized she was lucky enough to be married to a Data Scientist. Samiur created the program that scanned nonprofits’ websites collecting words. He also help make this website you’re reading right now. For more about Samiur, visit his website, or contact him on Twitter.

Also, many thanks to Sarah Healy and Maureen Feit.
